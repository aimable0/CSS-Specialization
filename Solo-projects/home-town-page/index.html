Top three activities to do at Gisenyi

Go swiming

Get refreshements at the kivu beach and also get to swim or learn how to with Giseny's community

Go hiking at Mont Rubavu

Do you love hiking, then hike the Mont Rubavu and be rewarded with a beautiful view of the plains of DR Congo

Get sweaty at the sports field

Play volleball, football, basketball or even lift some weights
